Creator of AI ADHD Coach Dopamind and art gallery philoart.io

The World As I See It (我的世界观)
en: The World As I See It

I’m a creator, a programmer, and a thinker.
I love building things from scratch—whether it’s a painting, a piece of code, or a deep-dive essay. For the longest time, however, the biggest obstacle to this passion was my own brain: I have ADHD.
After trying nearly every productivity tool on the market, I realized they were all designed for a neurotypical mind. Instead of helping, they left me feeling more anxious and self-critical.
It became clear that I didn’t need another complex “system” to manage. I needed a “partner”—something that understood me and could give me a gentle nudge right when I needed it most.
I couldn’t find it anywhere. So I decided to build it myself—for me, and for the millions of others with a “busy brain” just like mine.
That’s how Dopamind was born.
This blog is my personal space, a record of my explorations at the intersection of art, technology, and the human mind. Welcome.

我是一名创作者、程序员，也是一名思考者。
我热爱从零到一创造事物——无论是一幅画、一段代码，还是一篇深度思考的文章。但长久以来，这份热情最大的障碍，恰恰来自我的大脑——我有 ADHD。
在无数次尝试了市面上几乎所有的效率工具后，我发现它们大多是为“神经典型”大脑设计的，反而让我陷入更深的焦虑和自我怀疑。
我意识到，我需要的不是又一个复杂的管理“系统”，而是一个能理解我、在我最需要的时候轻推我一把的“伙伴”。
在任何地方都找不到它。于是，我决定亲手为自己，也为像我一样的数百万“繁忙大脑”，打造这样一个工具。
Dopamind由此诞生。
这个博客是我的个人世界，记录着我对艺术、科技和人类思维交叉点的探索。欢迎你。
